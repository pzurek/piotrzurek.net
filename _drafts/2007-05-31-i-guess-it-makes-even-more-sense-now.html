--- layout: post title: I guess it makes even more sense now tags: - Blog entries - GPS - Photo status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- So yesterday I blogged about how I don't understand why Picasa doesn't offer geotagging functionality and today Google is announcing the purchase of Panoramio - the service that allows for viewing geotagged photos in Google Maps and exporting Google Earth files. I'm probably not going to take too much risk predicting that the next version of Picasa will incorporate some kind of integration between pictures and GPS data. On a slightly related note, I uploaded some geotagged photos into Panoramio and the GPS data was not recognized - I was asked to place them on the map manually. Hopefully Google will iron out all the quirks...
